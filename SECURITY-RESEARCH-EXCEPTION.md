# Security research exception

Notwithstanding the [Polyform Noncommercial Licence](LICENSE), the author explicitly permits the use of this software for legitimate security research purposes. Given that the entire platform is a deliberately vulnerable ICS environment, this exception covers a lot of ground.

## Permitted activities

The following activities are explicitly allowed without a commercial licence, provided they are conducted in good faith:

- Vulnerability research and analysis
- Development of proof-of-concept exploits
- Defensive research and mitigation testing
- Red team and blue team exercises where no fee is charged
- Academic research and publication
- Incident response preparation and tabletop exercises
- Demonstrations intended to improve security awareness

This includes interaction with the platform over real network interfaces, protocol fuzzing, and external attack tooling.

## Responsible use

The exception applies to good-faith research. That means operating within environments you own or have permission to test, following responsible disclosure practices for any real-world findings, and not causing harm outside controlled lab conditions.

## What this exception does not allow

- Paid security services
- Commercial penetration testing or compliance auditing
- Consultancy engagements
- Training delivered for a fee

If research transitions into a paid engagement, a commercial licence is needed.

## Intent

The intent of this exception is to raise the level of security, not to restrict it. If you believe your use case aligns with this intent but falls into a grey area, [reach out](https://tymyrddin.dev/contact/).
